  
Aziz Haqqani is a senior member of the Haqqani Network and brother to Haqqani Network leader Sirajuddin Haqqani.  Aziz is intimately involved in logistical operations and command decisions in support of coordinating cross border attacks against International Security Assistance Force (ISAF) and Government of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan forces in Afghanistan.  Furthermore, he serves as the primary conduit (for the Haqqanis) for operations in Kabul and high profile attacks throughout the country.
  
Date of Birth: Circa 1988
